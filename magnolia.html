About Magnolia: What Happens here?
Magnolia is divided into two different sections. The right side of the building serves primarily as the hub for our science labs. It's worth noting that many students have recommended entering the classrooms from the exterior rather than using the indoor corridors.

The second part of Magnolia is primarily used for the humanities classes. These classes are predominantly located on the upper level of the building. To access this floor, simply enter through the main entrance of Magnolia and continue down the lengthy corridor. Once you reach the middle, you'll find a staircase to the left. Upstairs, you'll find the student counselling room and the humanities rooms. Downstairs, you will find the software design classroom, situated at the far end of the corridor on the right; Ms. Malik's office, located at the end of this corridor; and the high school office, which can be found to your right when you enter. If you have any inquiries related to high school matters, Ms. Homer, the high school secretary, is the person to contact. On the left side of the office, you'll find the high school hub, which serves as a convenient study spot during lunch and an excellent place to continue studying after school hours.
